Nathan Beck.

After a harrowing youth travelling around Europe with the circus, Nathan managed to run away at the age of 16 and joined a global shipping fleet based out of Calcutta, India. It was here that he picked up the guitar, learning from a fellow deckhand. After 2 years sailing around the world his guitar tutor and closest friend was accidentally killed in an terrible explosion in the Caribbean; it was then that Nathan decided it was best to return home to live with his aunt and uncle in Marple, South Manchester. From his uncle he picked up a healthy interest in digital design and marketing and eventually found himself working his way through the ranks to the creative corner desk at Bliss HQ, where he now gets to play around in Photoshop and churn out code every day to his hearts content.
